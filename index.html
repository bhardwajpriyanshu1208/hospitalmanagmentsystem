We are Covid safe

While these challenging yimes have brought many changes, at Star Hospital One thing remained the same - Our commitment to the Safety of our patients, their loved ones and our Staff.

We have established a safe environment and were among the first in the country to implemnet a range of rigorous protocols, which includes the following:

  • Extra Sanitation method

    • Disinfecting all areas and frequently touched surfaces continously.

  • Social Distancing

    • following Social distance protocols by implemneting limited visitation, new waiting room protocolsand other measures so you can safely pratice socail distancing and keep yourself and Others safe.

  • Face Mask

    • Mandating all patients and Staff to wear face masks.
